# measlespsa

Based on your reading of Booster Shots this semester, your task is to craft a short Public Service Announcement (PSA) that either 1/ raises awareness about the risks of measles and/or 2/ the importance of the measles vaccine. Your PSA can be any modality you wish: a flyer, a short TikTok/Youtube video (1-2 minutes max), a concept/pitch for a TV ad, etc. others ideas are welcome (just run them by one of the instructors). 

In addition the PSA itself, you must submit a 2 page (typed, double-spaced) minimum explanatory essay describing:

1. The intended message of the PSA.

2. Your intent/decision making process as to why you chose the medium you did.

3. How your PSA is directly informed by the material you read in Booster Shots. Please provide specific citations (using whichever standard style you are most comfortable with (MLA/APA/Chicago)) to the book in your explanatory essay.
